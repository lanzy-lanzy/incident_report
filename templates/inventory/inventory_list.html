Inventory Management Tips

Effective inventory management is crucial for disaster response. Here are some tips:

  • Regularly update inventory quantities to reflect actual stock levels.
  • Monitor distribution requests to anticipate resource needs.
  • Prioritize critical resources for immediate distribution.
  • Coordinate with suppliers to replenish depleted resources.
  • Maintain a buffer stock for emergency situations.
